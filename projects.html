Gamelan Nyai Cabe Rawit

Gamelan Nyai Cabe Rawit is currently in Poolewe, in Wester Ross on the edge of Loch Ewe. Offering locals the opportunity to learn to play traditional and new compositions for gamelan, this set of slendro instruments is on loan to the area for two years.
